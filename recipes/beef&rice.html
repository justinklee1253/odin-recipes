Gochujang Ground Beef & Rice

Description:

A really basic, but delicious meal to cook when you are short on time or feeling lazy.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb of Ground Beef.
  • Seasoning Seasoning
  • 3 tbsp of Gochujang.
  • 1/4 sliced up Onion
  • 2 Eggs

Steps:

  1. Get ingredients.
  2. Heat large cast-iron skillet on medium high heat.
  3. Season the Ground Beef with seasoning of your choice to taste.
  4. Toss in Ground Beef.
  5. After cooking 2-3 minutes, toss in Gochujang sauce and stir thoroughly until Ground Beef turns slightly red.

  7. Cook Eggs.
